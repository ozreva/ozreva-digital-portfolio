About Ozreva

Cybersecurity Graduate | Enterprise Security Practitioner | Continuous Learner

Professional Journey

Over the past two years, I have developed a strong passion for cybersecurity and enterprise IT infrastructures, earning an Associate’s degree in Information Technology as well as a Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity. With an increased interest in Active Directory security, network defense, and security operations, I have successfully completed numerous hands-on labs and projects designed to replicate real-world challenges encountered by organizations.

My approach combines technical rigor with pragmatic solutions that balance security, usability, and operational feasibility.

  • Hands-on experience with pfSense, Suricata, Splunk, Kali Linux, Mimikatz, and more

Philosophy and Vision

I firmly believe cybersecurity is a continuous journey. Staying ahead of emerging threats requires persistent learning, hands-on practice, and a mindset of defense in depth. I am committed to leveraging technology and collaboration to build resilient systems that safeguard critical data and services.

Looking Forward
