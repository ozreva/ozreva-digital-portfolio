Certifications

Professional credentials that validate my skills and knowledge in cybersecurity and networking.

CompTIA Security+

The CompTIA Security+ certification is a globally recognized credential that validates foundational skills in IT security. It covers essential topics such as network security, threats and vulnerabilities, identity management, cryptography, and risk management, equipping professionals to secure organizations effectively.

CompTIA Network+

The CompTIA Network+ certification verifies the knowledge and skills necessary to design, configure, manage, and troubleshoot wired and wireless networks. It serves as a critical stepping stone for networking professionals and lays a solid foundation for advanced security certifications.
